About Us

Welcome to our mental health chatbot! Designed to offer reliable information and resources, our chatbot is here to help you improve your mental well-being.

Seeking help can be challenging, but we aim to make it accessible and comfortable for everyone. Please note, however, that this chatbot is not a replacement for professional help.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise mental health awareness by providing accurate and up-to-date information. We offer tools, such as mental health exercises and evaluations, to support your well-being.

Our Team

This chatbot was developed by a passionate student committed to breaking the stigma around mental health and creating a supportive community. Thank you for choosing us in your journey towards better mental health!
